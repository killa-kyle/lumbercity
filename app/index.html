About Us

Lumber City & Building Supply is a neighborhood Lumber yard serving Williamsburg for the past 12 years. We have made our home here and welcome you to stop in for anything you may need for your home or building project. We aim to please every customer that walks in our doors. Whether it be a Large commercial contractor in the industry for 30 years or someone who has never held a hammer and nail, we want to help. Friendly service and a full line of products makes us accomplish these goals day in and day out.
